Hackathon Club

A club where students are able to unleash their potential in programming by participating in hackathons and coding competitions.

Get Started

What's the purpose?

This club was created to help other people get introduced to the beauty of coding competitions. Furthermore, it is a great way for people to get introduced to other coders to form a team or work a new project.

hackathons

A hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, and others, often including domain experts, collaborate intensively on software projects

Game jams

A game jam is a hackathon for video games. It is a gathering of people for the purpose of planning, designing, and creating one or more games within a short span of time, usually ranging between 24 and 72 hours, and some even longer. Participants are generally made up of programmers, game designers, artists, writers, and others in game development-related fields.

Competitive programming

A programming competition generally involves the host presenting a set of logical or mathematical problems to the contestants.

Many Other Types

There are many types of programming competitions other than the ones mentioned above. Open source, data science, and engineering competitions these are but a few of the types of coding competitions one could participate in.

Our plan

This club began as a way to introduce people to competitive programming. However for one to start entering competitions he needs to learn how to program.

I know how to program

You should still join the club. There will be competitions form time to time which will greatly help you in the future. Also you could form a team with some other members who know how to program and enter competitions. There will always be something for you to do.
